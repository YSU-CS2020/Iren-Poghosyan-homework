Dataset: Wine Quality (Red Wine)
Source:  UCI Machine Learning Repository
URL:     https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/wine+quality
File:    https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/machine-learning-databases/wine-quality/winequality-red.csv

Description:
  The dataset contains physicochemical properties of red Vinho Verde wine samples
  from northern Portugal, alongside sensory quality scores (3–8) assigned by wine experts.

  For this assignment the task is binarised:
    label 0 → low quality  (quality < 6)
    label 1 → high quality (quality >= 6)

Samples:  1,599
Features: 11 (all numerical)
  1.  fixed acidity
  2.  volatile acidity
  3.  citric acid
  4.  residual sugar
  5.  chlorides
  6.  free sulfur dioxide
  7.  total sulfur dioxide
  8.  density
  9.  pH
  10. sulphates
  11. alcohol

Classes:  2 (binary, approx. 46% low / 54% high after binarisation)

Citation:
  P. Cortez, A. Cerdeira, F. Almeida, T. Matos and J. Reis.
  "Modeling wine preferences by data mining from physicochemical properties."
  Decision Support Systems, 47(4):547-553, 2009.
